About Me

Introduction

I'm a devoted, hard-working guy who is goal and detail oriented. Although I've been a successful chef for the past 17 years, I decided it was time for a career change. I am now a full-time student in Thinkful's Front-End Web Development Career Path program. Before committing to the Thinkful program, I dabbled in coding at several free online coding websites and have taken several college level coding courses.

I have a beautiful wife and a 5 year old son, both of which are the most important parts of my life.

Where I'm From

I grew up in Idaho but have lived all over the western United States, with a stint on the gulf coast of Florida. My two favorite places to live are the wine country of California and in the shadows of the Tetons in Jackson, Wyoming.
